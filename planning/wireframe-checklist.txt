
3.2 User Stories
Rendezvous implements these user stories. A member can:

3.2.1 Member Stories
Connect to Rendezvous using an email address and password. 
Set core hours for each day of the week.
View, add, update, and remove commitments.

3.2.2 Team Stories
Create a team.
Join one or more teams.
Leave a team.

3.2.3 Activity Stories
1. Propose an activity
2. Vote to accept or reject time slots for a proposed activity.
3. Schedule an activity proposed by that member by selecting from time slots accepted by the entire team.
4. View a schedule of proposed activities and their confirmation status.
5. View a schedule of confirmed activities.
